## Out-of-scope for this transcript

The firmware build, flash, and serial banner read (steps 4–7 of
`infra/scripts/tests/manual/remote-host-tdongle.md`) require
pip-installing esptool + platformio on the Pi (~3-5 min), the
PlatformIO espressif32 toolchain download (~500 MB, several
minutes), the firmware build, and a write to the devices flash.
Those steps validate agent-driven workflow over the substrate
mechanism, not the substrate mechanism itself; the substrate
mechanism is fully validated by the registration / verification /
reachability above plus the docker-loopback Phase 17 test. The
firmware-flash run is the operators next step against the
documented manual procedure.
